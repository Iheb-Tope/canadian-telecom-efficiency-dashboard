# Canadian Telecom: Revenue & Workforce Efficiency (2020–2024)

A Tableau dashboard analyzing Canadian telecommunications industry performance with a focus on workforce efficiency and productivity.

## Tools
- Microsoft Excel (data cleaning, KPI calculations)
- Tableau Public (dashboard, parameter-driven year selection)

## Data Source
Statistics Canada (Telecommunications industry; NAICS 517 / 5173).

## KPIs
- Total Revenue (CAD, millions) + YoY %
- Total Employees
- Revenue per Employee (CAD)
- Productivity Gap (%) = Revenue YoY % − Employment YoY %

## Key Insights
- Revenue remained stable while employment declined in 2023–2024, increasing revenue per employee.
- The YoY Growth Quadrant highlights years where revenue growth outpaced workforce growth (efficiency gain zone).
